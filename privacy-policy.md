Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 22, 2026

Shree Ram Shalaka ("we," "our," or "app") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy outlines how your information is managed when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect
No Personal Data Collected by Us: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

Local Storage on Device: Any inputs, saved verses, reading history, journal entries, or user preferences (such as font size and notification settings) are stored exclusively on your local device storage. This data is never sent to our servers or any external parties.

2. Third-Party Services and Advertising
To keep this app free of charge and support maintenance, we use Google AdMob for serving advertisements.

Google AdMob: Google AdMob may collect and process certain non-personally identifiable data, such as your device's Advertising ID, device type, network information, and approximate location, to deliver relevant ads, prevent fraud, and measure ad performance.

User Control: You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting the ad settings on your mobile device (e.g., Settings -> Privacy -> Ads).

3. In-App Purchases and Billing
If the app offers optional in-app purchases or donations, all financial transactions are processed securely through the Google Play Billing System. We do not collect, store, or have access to your credit card, bank details, or financial payment information.

4. Data Retention and Security
Because your personal entries (questions, journal notes, and saved verses) remain entirely on your device, you have full control over your data. Clearing app data or uninstalling the app will permanently erase all locally saved information.

5. Children's Privacy
Our app is suitable for all age groups. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Our third-party ad providers comply with relevant children's privacy regulations (including COPPA and GDPR).

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
